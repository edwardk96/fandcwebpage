Why Founders & Coders?

  • I started teaching myself a year ago, completing CS50 and working through the Odin Project, W3 Schools and FreeCodeCamp.
  • I would really benefit from an in person programme to ask, learn and share best practises – the FAC workshop showed me the power of harnessing many diverse brains instead of one.
  • Want to be part of and actively contribute to a community of self-starters and would be proud to be able to give back.
  • AI has lowered the barriers to coding, but now more than ever it’s a no brainer to build solid foundations.
  • The founders component really appealed to me and the day a week structure would be most compatible with trying to start a business.
