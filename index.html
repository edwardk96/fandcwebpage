About Me

  • Hi, I’m Eddie.
  • Originally from Yorkshire, I came to London 10 years ago.
  • I’ve spent 7 years in corporate jobs (🥱) and now feel ready for the next chapter / challenge.
  • I’m slightly one-dimensional, as most of my free time is spent running, regularly competing in 100km ultra marathons.
  • Who doesn’t say this but… I also love a good book, baking, and a sunny Sunday afternoon pint.

Why Coding?

  • Firstly, I genuinely just really enjoy it (admittedly there are also many hours where I pull my hair out…).
  • I love the possibility of being able to build anything from a blank canvas – it feels like Lego, which I obsessed over as a kid.
  • Secondly, having just given notice on my job, I plan to try and start my own business, having worked on, but not yet fully dedicated myself, to several projects in the last 12 months.
  • Whilst I’ve met and have experience working with engineers, I want to be able to understand, emphasise and contribute more; and have the skills to build things myself.

Why Founders & Coders?

  • I started teaching myself a year ago, completing CS50 and working through the Odin Project, W3 Schools and FreeCodeCamp.
  • I would really benefit from an in person programme to ask, learn and share best practises – the FAC workshop showed me the power of harnessing many diverse brains instead of one.
  • Want to be part of and actively contribute to a community of self-starters, and I would be proud to have the skills to give back.
  • AI has lowered the barriers to coding, but now more than ever it’s a no brainer to build solid foundations.
  • The Founders component of FAC appealed to me and the course structure and community would be most compatible with trying to start a business.

I appreciate my application may cause issues (e.g. for FAC’s funding model) as right now I’m not sure whether I would be applying for an apprenticeship, nonetheless, I am fully committed to finishing the course, would actively participate and help others, and put my new skills to good use.
